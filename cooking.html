Cooking Rice

Here's how to cook different types of rice perfectly.

Long-Grain Rice (e.g., Basmati)

Long-grain rice like basmati needs to be rinsed before cooking to get rid of any excess starch and then boiled or cooked by the absorption method.

Brown Rice

Brown rice needs more water than white rice to cook and takes longer. Soaking it in cold water for at least 30 minutes and up to several hours will help cut the cooking time. To use the method below, add 1¼ times the amount of water.

Short-Grain Rice (e.g., Arborio)

Short-grain rice for risotto, paella, and rice pudding need the excess starch to make the final dish creamy, so don’t rinse it. For these dishes, the rice is cooked along with the other ingredients.

Cooking Times

  • Basmati and Long-Grain Rice: About 20 minutes from start to finish on the stovetop, but basmati cooks best if soaked for 20 minutes before.
  • Brown Rice: Takes around 30 to 40 minutes on the stove.
  • Short-Grain Rice: Cooking time depends on the specific dish, generally 20-25 minutes for dishes like risotto.
  • Oven Method: Around 35 minutes for basmati rice.
  • Microwave Method: About 14 minutes for basmati rice, including 6 minutes on high power and an extra 8 minutes on medium power.

Rice-to-Water Ratio

For basmati and all kinds of white rice, use a ratio of 1:2 when cooking on the stove. So, for every 1 cup of rice, add 2 cups of water.

How to Cook Rice on the Stove

  1. Measure the rice and water.

  2. Rinse the rice under cold water to remove excess starch.

  3. Soak the rice in cold water for at least 30 minutes (optional).

  4. Bring rice and water to a boil, then simmer covered for 10 minutes.

  5. Remove from heat and let stand for 5-10 minutes before fluffing with a fork.

Other Cooking Methods

If the stove method is not available or preferred, rice can also be cooked in the oven or microwave.

How to Cook Rice in the Oven

  1. Preheat oven to fan 160C/conventional 180C/gas 4.

  2. Rinse rice, then place in a casserole dish with boiling water.

  3. Cover and bake for 35 minutes.

  4. Let stand for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.

How to Cook Rice in the Microwave

  1. Place rice in a heatproof bowl and top with boiling water.

  2. Cover with microwaveable wrap and pierce several times.

  3. Cook on high (900W) for 6 minutes, then medium (500W) for 8 minutes.

  4. Let stand for a few minutes before serving.

How to Cook Simple Curry

Here's a step-by-step guide to cooking a simple curry:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.

  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.

  3. Add crushed garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute.

  4. Add your choice of protein (chicken, tofu, or vegetables) and cook until browned.

  5. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and coriander powder, and cook for a minute to release the flavors.

  6. Pour in coconut milk or tomato sauce and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.

  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
